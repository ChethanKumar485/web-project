About Roarstar Deep Fake Detector

Roarstar Deep Fake Detector is an AI-powered deepfake detection system developed to combat the growing threat of synthetic media on social platforms. Our multimodal approach analyzes multiple indicators to provide accurate detection results.

The system was trained on datasets including DFDC (Deepfake Detection Challenge) and DFD (Deep Fake Detection) to recognize the latest generation of deepfake techniques.
